# Product Requirements Document

## Introduction
$PROJECT_NAME is a student-targeted companion application meant to streamline the studying process by ingesting PDF documents and turning them into usable flashcards.

## Impact
Prioritizes studying over creating study materials, while offering organizational features to group related flashcard sets in an intuitive interface.

## Primary Issues
1. Students often spend significant time creating flashcards from lecture notes or textbooks, taking away from meaningful time that could be spent studying instead.
2. Traditional study materials (like paper flashcards) lack portability and convenience, making it challenging to study in various environments.

## Target Users
1. Students
- Need quick study material generation
- Desire intuitive interface
- Require organizational capability
